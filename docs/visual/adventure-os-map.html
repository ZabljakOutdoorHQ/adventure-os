The rules of the climb

Four promises the whole thing is built on

01

Read before write

Every integration starts read-only. Controlled writes come only after the contract is understood and tested.

02

Source & confidence, always

Every fact shows where it came from and whether it's confirmed, probable or just suggested.

03

No silent authority

AI may search, connect and propose. Sending, publishing, moving money or changing bookings needs your explicit yes.

04

Your tools stay yours

Existing systems remain authoritative. Adventure OS is a lens over them, never a replacement that traps your data.
